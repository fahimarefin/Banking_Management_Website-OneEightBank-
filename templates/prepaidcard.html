One-Eight PREPAID CARDS

One-Eight is the pioneer in introducing technology based financial solutions in Bangladesh. Over the years it has emerged as the largest plastic card issuer in the market with wide array of Debit and Credit Cards. However, there are many customers who are not eligible to get a Credit Card or do not want to go through the conventional bank account opening process to get a Debit Card; but they want to enjoy all the facilities of a Card. Keeping the demand of these customer segments in mind, the bank has launched the following 6 types of Prepaid Cards. Prepaid cards are considered to be more convenient and adaptable for consumers than paper or other electronic instruments like mobile wallets or virtual accounts because of their ease of use and acceptability at thousands of merchants.
